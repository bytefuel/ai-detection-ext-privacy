Privacy Policy for AI Text Detector

Effective Date: 01/03/2025

Introduction

AI Text Detector (the "Extension") is a Chrome browser extension designed to detect and analyze whether text from LinkedIn posts is AI-generated. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information while using our Extension.

1. Information We Collect

The Extension collects the following types of information:

  • Text Data: The text content of LinkedIn posts is sent to our backend server for processing to determine if the text is AI-generated.

2. How We Use the Information

The collected text data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Determining whether the text is AI-generated.
  • Returning results to the user within the Extension interface.

3. Data Storage and Retention

The text data is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties. Once the analysis is complete, the data is discarded immediately.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your information to third parties.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the data transmitted between the Extension and our backend servers.

6. User Consent

By using the Extension, you consent to the processing of LinkedIn post text data as described in this privacy policy.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy as needed. Changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
